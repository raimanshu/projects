Description: Glassmorphism is a design trend that mimics the appearance of frosted glass. It typically features a semi-transparent background with a blurred effect, layered over colorful or vibrant backgrounds. The effect gives a sense of depth and layering. Key Features: Blurred Backgrounds: Use CSS properties like backdrop-filter: blur(10px); and background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.3); to create a frosted glass effect. Transparency: Semi-transparent layers to allow background colors and shapes to show through. Soft Shadows and Borders: Use subtle shadows and border-radius to enhance the glassy look. Example: Ideal for card designs, modals, and overlays on colorful or gradient backgrounds.
