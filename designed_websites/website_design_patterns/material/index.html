Description: Developed by Google, Material Design uses principles of physical objects in a digital space, with an emphasis on grid-based layouts, responsive animations, and depth effects like shadows and lighting. Key Features: Depth and Elevation: Use of shadows to indicate elevation and layering of elements. Bold Colors: Use of vibrant, contrasting colors along with consistent typography. Responsive Animations: Smooth, intuitive animations that respond to user interactions. Example: Widely used in Android apps and websites to create a cohesive and intuitive user experience.
